How QualiProof Works

A simple four-step process to transform your quality assurance

1

Connect Your NDT Equipment

Integrate QualiProof with your existing non-destructive testing systems using our simple APIs and SDKs.

2

Local AI Processing

Our f-AnoGAN model runs locally on your facility's data to detect defects while keeping all sensitive information private.

3

Contribute to Federated Learning

Share only model improvements (not raw data) to help the global detection model become smarter for all participants.

4

Receive Certification & Rewards

Get blockchain-based quality certificates for compliant parts and earn QualiProof tokens for your contributions.
